Plan Details

Basic Plan

Our Basic Plan is designed to provide essential coverage at an affordable cost. It covers emergency services, doctor's visits, prescription drugs, and health screenings. This plan is suitable for individuals and families looking for budget-friendly healthcare options.

Standard Plan

The Standard Plan offers moderate coverage, including emergency services, specialist consultations, maternity care, and dental check-ups. It's a well-rounded plan suitable for those seeking comprehensive healthcare without the highest premium costs.

Premium Plan

Our Premium Plan provides comprehensive coverage for complete peace of mind. It includes all the features of the Basic and Standard plans, along with dental and vision care, wellness programs, and 24/7 customer support. This plan is ideal for individuals and families who want the highest level of healthcare coverage.
